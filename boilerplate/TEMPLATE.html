Brewing

For brewing this coffee I am using the Kalita wave. The defining characteristics of the Kalita Wave are that it is a flat bottom cone with three small holes in the bottom. In my experiences with coffee drippers, the Kalita provides the consistency between brews, while bringing a nice balance of body and acidity.

When dialing in our coffees, we use a refractometer to measure how effectively we are extracting the coffee. By measuring our extractions, we are able to adjust our brewing variables accordingly, bringing forward the best possible cup of coffee.
